Our Mission

Glow & Grow crafts high-performance hair and skin care powered by nature and validated by science. We formulate with gentle actives and cold-pressed botanicals to restore barrier health, boost shine, and deliver glow—without compromise.

What We Stand For

  • Visible results with minimal routines
  • Clean, cruelty-free, ethically sourced ingredients
  • Inclusive care for all hair textures & skin types
  • Planet-first packaging & responsible production

Our Ingredients

We favor biomimetic ceramides, peptides, niacinamide, plant oils (marula, baobab, argan), and antioxidant-rich extracts (green tea, licorice, sea buckthorn). Every supplier signs our “No List” and traceability pledge.

Our Story

Glow & Grow began with a simple idea: everyday care should be effortless and effective. After years of formulating for friends and family with sensitive skin and diverse hair textures, we launched a line that puts barrier health and scalp balance first.

Today, our community inspires every product we create—from multitasking serums to restorative masks— each designed to fit real life and real schedules.

Our Values

  • Transparency: full INCI lists and clear percentages for hero actives.
  • Safety: toxicologist-reviewed, dermatologist-tested, sensitive-skin first.
  • Sustainability: glass where possible, PCR plastics, refill pilots in progress.
  • Inclusion: tested across curls, coils, waves, and straight hair; all skin tones.

Research & Testing

Each formula goes through stability, micro, and panel testing. We publish claim substantiation where applicable and update formulas as new evidence emerges.

What We Never Use

  • SLS/SLES, formaldehyde donors, hydroquinone
  • Undisclosed fragrance allergens
  • Animal testing at any stage
